What do we offer?

DrinkIt is a product that will help you on a daily basis to keep your body optimally hydrated. This bottle is equipped with external sensors that enable it to detect your hydration level in order to advise you as best as possible according to your current state. It will also be able to adapt to the nature of the liquid it contains in order to modify its recommendations according to the drink you wish to drink. In addition, it has the ability to purify water from various sources.
